Lord Shiva

"One in whom the whole creation sleeps after dissolution"

Lord Shiva Animated Visualization (All credits to the designer/artist)

Few Characteristics of Lord Shiva -:

  • Rudra: Fierce, destructive, One who can kill the forces of darkness.
  • Third eye: with which he burned Desire to ashes.
  • Ashes: Smears his body with ashes; represents the end of all material existence.
  • Blue throat (Neelkanth): Drank the poison to eliminate its destructive capacity and save whole life form.
  • Sacred Ganga: Represents the nectar of immortality.
  • Deer: Holding deer on one hand; indicates he has removed randomness of mind, attained maturity and firmness in thought process.
  • Trident: Held in His right hand; represents the three Gunas— Sattva, Rajas and Tamas. That is the emblem of sovereignty.

Destroyer and Benefactor, Ascetic and Householder, God of Ambiguity and Paradox.

"One should know all things of the phenomenal world as of a fivefold character, for the reason that the eternal verity of Śiva is of the character of the fivefold Brahman."

Pañcabrahma Upanishad 31
